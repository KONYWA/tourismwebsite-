Discover Zanzibar: Our Top Tours

Explore the best of Zanzibar with our curated tours. From rich history to exotic beaches, embark on a journey that captures the island’s natural beauty and vibrant culture.

Stone Town Tour

Stone Town, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, offers an enchanting blend of cultures, visible in its narrow alleys and architectural marvels. Explore the Old Fort, marvel at the intricate doors of Arabic influence, and visit the House of Wonders. This tour offers an in-depth experience of Zanzibar's rich history, including the birthplace of Freddie Mercury. Enjoy markets filled with spices, crafts, and a fusion of Swahili, Arab, and Indian culture.

Location: Stone Town, Zanzibar

Safari Blue Tour

Safari Blue is a one-day ocean adventure perfect for those wanting to experience Zanzibar's marine treasures. Sail on a traditional dhow, snorkel among vibrant coral reefs, and relax on the untouched Kwale Island. You’ll also enjoy a seafood feast with tropical fruits. This tour is ideal for families and adventurers eager to see Zanzibar's sparkling waters and rich marine life, including dolphins if you're lucky!

Location: Fumba Village, Zanzibar

Spice Farm Tour

Known as the “Spice Island,” Zanzibar's Spice Farm Tour offers an aromatic journey through spice plantations. Walk through fields of cloves, nutmeg, vanilla, and cinnamon while learning about their culinary and medicinal uses. Engage with local farmers and enjoy freshly brewed spiced tea and tropical fruits. This tour is ideal for all ages and offers a glimpse into the backbone of Zanzibar’s economy.

Location: Kizimbani, Zanzibar

Prison Island Tour

Originally intended as a prison, Prison Island has become a sanctuary for Aldabra giant tortoises. Located a short boat ride from Stone Town, the island offers snorkeling opportunities and a chance to encounter tortoises over 100 years old. Stroll through historic buildings and relax on sandy beaches. A perfect mix of history and nature, ideal for those interested in conservation and marine life.

Location: Changuu Island, Zanzibar

Nakupenda Beach Tour

Meaning "I Love You" in Swahili, Nakupenda Beach is a stunning sandbank offering crystal-clear waters and soft sands. Enjoy a day of swimming, sunbathing, and seafood dining in a beautiful natural setting. The tour includes boat rides, snorkeling, and fresh tropical fruit tastings. Nakupenda Beach is a top destination for those looking to relax in paradise with a touch of adventure.

Location: Nakupenda Sandbank, Zanzibar

Jozani Forest Tour

Jozani Forest is home to the rare Red Colobus monkey and an array of unique flora and fauna. This tour takes you through the lush mangrove forests and ancient mahogany trees, where you’ll encounter diverse wildlife and learn about local conservation efforts. Ideal for nature lovers, Jozani Forest offers a rare glimpse into Zanzibar's ecological beauty and biodiversity.

Location: Jozani Chwaka Bay National Park, Zanzibar
